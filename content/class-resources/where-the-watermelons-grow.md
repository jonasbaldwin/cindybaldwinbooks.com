## MG at Heart

_Where the Watermelons Grow_ was the featured book for our August 2018 edition of the Middle Grade at Heart Newsletter! You can download a [PDF of the issue](/class-resources/watermelonsGrowTeachingGuide.pdf). This discussion guide, designed with classrooms in mind, features an exclusive interview, discussion questions, a recipe for honey-sweetened watermelon limeade, an activity, and a printable coloring page.
